英美当代畅销小说评析

2021-01-16
-
笔记-英语

英美当代畅销小说评析笔记总结。

Chapter 6

furious: very angry

​ 近似词 rage: road rage

now: 可以表示情绪；可以作为一种愤怒的语气词

cascade: 大大的滴落；

ceiling: 天花板；

​ 引申为上限

“What the hell did you think you were doing, Sara”

the hell 是强调 What 类似的结构还有 on earth passenger seat: 指副驾驶

slam: slam dunk 扣篮

“Please don’t tell”

tell: 告发

dribble: 落下；（篮球、足球）的运球

sarcasm: 讽刺，挖苦

“Keep your hands where we can see them and get out real slow”

real = really

slow = slowly

​ 这俩个作副词用时一般放在动词后面

throw up = vomit = 呕吐

reach in = 伸手进去（够）

good: 有充足（理由）的

​ good reason/idea

take over = 占据上风

“I yelled, “GUN!” a split second before she shot me.”

split second: 表示时间很短

Chapter 7

distinct:

​ 能被清楚的听见/看见/感觉到

​ 与……截然不同/界限分明

flinch = 由于疼痛本能的突然做出动作

even as = at the same time as

register:

​ to show or express a feeling

“They’d forgotten about me”

forget about me 忽视忽略

“I felt for my 9mm Glock”

feel for 摸索

mm: mili-metre

level: 水平面；

​ 表示举枪对着

etch: 蚀刻

​ 引申为脸上表现表情（很明显那种）

“Her face was etched with fear”

shoot sb 射杀

shoot at sb 朝某人射击

Chapter 8

hush: 🤫 quiet/speaking very quietly

call out = 大喊

call in

​ 召集

​ 给单位打电话（比如请假）

siren = 警笛

“I unhooked my Nextel from my belt and, to the best of my ability, I called in.”

to the best of sb’s knowledge 竭尽……所知

civilian & police and army

dispatcher 调度员

“The sounds were fading in and out.”

fade in/out （声音）淡入淡出

pool 池塘； （液体）汇集；抽象的汇集汇聚

“I played the whole thing over in my mind”

over (again) 重复

cuff 袖口（卡夫)；handcuff 手铐

​ 这里作动词表示用手铐拷上

frisk 搜身

“throwing the kids against the car”

throw = move sth with force

inexcusably (excuse) = 不可原谅地

close over 笼罩

competent 有足够的经验技能做某事

Chapter 9

nondescript = dull

legitimate = 合理的

survey

​ surveill = 监视

no bigger than = 和什么差不多大（强调小）

gig = gigabyte

faint = 模糊的

“Everyone was right on time.”

right 强调 on time

SUV = sport utility vehicle

in the disguise of

count on sb to do/doing sth

Chapter 10

haze = 阴霾；雾霾

“I came awake with a jolt” 惊醒

“A dark face loomed over me, lit from behind with a hazy white glow”

loom over 突然出现，让人害怕

loom large 逼近，迫近（战争逼近了）

“The fog was lifting”

lift 消散

make it = to survive after a serious illness or accident

Washington DC = Distict of Cplumbia

pager = bleeper 传呼机

Chapter 11

saint = 圣人/好人/善良人

secure = to obtain / achieve sth after a lot effort

​ secure a bank loan on sth （用什么做抵押贷款）

ICU = Intensive Care Unit

shade = 色度

eggplant = 茄子

“This was my third visit in as many days”

as many days 每天都是相同的次数

unrelenting = 不断的，不减的

wince = to suddenly make an expression with

bud = buddy

“It sucks, Boxer.”

sth suck ↔ sth rock

“Would I hesitate when I shouldn’t? Shoot before thinking? “

这里的 when 表示 even though something is true，即是说我会不会在我本不应该犹豫的时候犹豫

shoulda = should’ve = should have

​ coulda, woulda…

off-duty clothes 便装

catch sb = 碰巧遇到

“He looked like a teenager coming to pick up his first date”

pick up 接约会对象

​ pick up 重新获得

prints = 指纹

swear = 发誓

​ swear in 总统宣誓就职

​ I swear on my mother’s head 我拿我妈的头做担保发誓

runaway = 儿童（离家出走的）

“Don’t worry about nothin’

双重否定表示肯定

Hall of Justice 警察总部（常常设有司法机关）

forge = 锻造；引申为加强

harbor = 港口；庇护；（引申为）怀有一个想法（尤其是负面的）
